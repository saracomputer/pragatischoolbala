Mahalaxmi English High School

Faculty

Principal/Headmaster/Headmistress:

  • overall leadership and administration of the school
  • Developing and implementing school policies and procedures
  • Supervising staff and evaluating their performance
  • Managing budgets and resources
  • Building relationships with parents, community members, and stakeholders

Vice Principal/Assistant Principal:

  • Assisting the principal in administrative duties
  • Managing discipline and student behavior
  • Overseeing curriculum implementation
  • Supporting teachers and staff
  • Assisting with the day-to-day operations of the school

Teachers/Instructors

  • Planning and delivering lessons based on curriculum standards
  • Assessing student progress and providing feedback
  • Creating a positive and inclusive classroom environment
  • Managing classroom behavior and maintaining discipline
  • Communicating with parents/guardians about student performance

Department Heads

  • Leading a specific subject area or department within the school
  • Coordinating curriculum development and implementation
  • Mentoring and supporting teachers in the department
  • Collaborating with other department heads and school administration
  • Ensuring alignment with educational standards and goals

School Counselors

  • Providing guidance and counseling services to students
  • Assisting students with academic planning and goal setting
  • Supporting students with personal and social challenges
  • Conducting career exploration and assisting with college applications
  • Collaborating with teachers, parents, and outside agencies for student support

Librarians

  • Managing the school library and its resources
  • Assisting students and staff with research and information needs
  • Promoting literacy and reading programs
  • Organizing library events and activities
  • Maintaining the library collection and cataloging materials

Special Education Teachers

  • Teaching and supporting students with disabilities or special needs
  • Developing individualized education plans (IEPs)
  • Adapting instructional strategies and materials
  • Collaborating with other teachers and specialists
  • Monitoring and assessing student progress

School Psychologists

  • Conducting psychological assessments and evaluations
  • Providing counseling and support for students' emotional well-being
  • Collaborating with teachers and parents to address students' learning or behavioral challenges
  • Assisting in the development of intervention plans
  • Promoting positive mental health and well-being within the school community
